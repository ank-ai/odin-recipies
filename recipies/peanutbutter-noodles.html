Peanutbutter Noodles

description:

Peanutbutter noodles are very easy to make.

total time for preaparation and cooking: 10 to 15 minutes

ingredients:

  • cucumber
  • peanutbutter
  • soy sauce
  • garlic
  • noodles, preferably white chinese noodles
  • chinese vinegar (optional)

steps:

  1. you can start by bringing a pot of water to a boil
  2. while the water is heating up, chop up some cucumber slices
  3. cut up the garlic into small pieces
  4. add peanutbutter in a bowl and slowly add water while mixing
  5. mix peanutbutter until it is smooth and has the consistency of ketchup
  6. cook your noodles
  7. mix chopped garlic with the peanutbutter sauce
  8. add soy sauce and vinegar into the peanutbutter bowl to taste
  9. once the noodles are cooked through, drain the water and combine with peanutbutter sauce
  10. add cucumber
