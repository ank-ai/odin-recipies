Mashed Tofu

description:

It may not look appealing, but it's very easy to make and tastes pretty damn good!

ingredients:

  • spring onions
  • soy sauce
  • semsame oil
  • soft white tofu

steps:

  1. chop up some spring onions
  2. drain the tofu and add it into a bowl with the spring onions
  3. mix the tofu until it has the consistency of porridge
  4. add soy sauce and seseme oil, a table spoon each
  5. salt to taste
