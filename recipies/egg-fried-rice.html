Egg Fried Rice

description:

Egg fried rice is a common dish eaten all over asia.

Of course the taste and ingredients vary from country to country and region to region.

This recipe version is based off of chinese egg fried rice.

ingredients:

  • 2 bowls of rice (preferably from the day before)
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 piece of ginger (garlic clove size)
  • 1/2 onion
  • salt
  • soy sauce
  • spring onion (to garnish)

steps:

  1. chop garlic, ginger and onion into small pieces
  2. break two eggs into a bowl and mix until homogenous
  3. pour the eggs into hot oil and fry until it becomes solid, try to stir during this step
  4. take out the eggs from the pan, then add garlic, onion and ginger and fry until the garlic starts to brown
  5. throw in the rice and eggs and start mixing
  6. add a table spoon of soy sauce
  7. salt to taste
  8. add chopped spring onion as garnish (optional)
